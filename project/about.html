Our Mission

To infuse children with the warmth and spirit of Chabad Lubavitch values.

We accomplish this through a high-quality curriculum taught by dedicated teachers, hands-on activities, and extracurricular programming — in an atmosphere of true Ahavas Yisrael and Chassidishkeit.

Our aim is to nurture, inspire, and empower students to reach personal excellence. Within a warm environment, we cultivate a love for learning, guide children in developing academic and social skills, and instill true confidence in each individual child.

A Local Setting

Closer to home, within a more settled environment.

Children benefit from a stable and manageable daily routine. Cheder Menachem provides a local option for families in Inverrary and the surrounding community — allowing children to begin their Cheder experience without the long commute that often weighs on a young child's day.

At this age, children are taught in an appropriate, structured setting, with the long-term goal of developing into separate boys' and girls' tracks as the program grows.

Our Approach

The approach found in established Chabad מוסדות — adapted for young children.

The classroom is built around סדר (structure), בהירות (clarity), and עקביות (consistency). Alongside this, the environment is warm and respectful, allowing each child to feel comfortable while being guided with clear expectations.

This reflects a long-tested approach: that when expectations are clear and the day is predictable, children settle in quickly — and the work of teaching becomes the work of nurturing growth rather than managing disorder. It is the approach families recognize from the established Chabad moisdos, scaled and softened appropriately for this age.
