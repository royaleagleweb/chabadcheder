Welcome

A program for families who want the right beginning.

ברוכים הבאים

Cheder Menachem of Inverrary serves families in the local community with a single, carefully built Pre-1A class. The program is designed to give children a proper start to their first year of Cheder — in a structured, steady environment, guided by the values and approach of established Chabad educational institutions.

A Strong Foundation

The early years are the foundation for everything that follows.

In Chabad chinuch, this stage is understood not only for what a child learns, but for the steady approach to learning, behavior, and routine that he or she carries forward.

Our work at Pre-1A is to build that approach quietly and consistently — so the child arrives at first grade prepared, settled, and ready to grow.

The Approach

Three guiding principles in the classroom.

Alongside structure, the environment is warm and respectful — allowing each child to feel comfortable while being guided with clear expectations.

סֵדֶר

Structure

A consistent rhythm to the day. Children learn what comes next and settle into the routine.

בְּהִירוּת

Clarity

Clear expectations, clearly given. Each child understands what is being asked and is set up to succeed.

עַקְבִיּוּת

Consistency

The same calm direction, day after day — the approach found in established Chabad מוסדות.

Learning Focus

Building a proper approach to learning from the very start.

  • Foundational לימודי קודש
  • Early development of learning habits
  • Focus on middos and behavior
  • A balanced structure of learning and play

At the Pre-1A stage, the program focuses on establishing strong foundational skills within the core curriculum. English language skills are introduced at the appropriate stage in a child's development, based on guidance from experienced educators.
