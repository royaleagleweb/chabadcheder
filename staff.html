Mrs. Chaya Katz

Early childhood educator

Mrs. Katz brings many years of experience working with young children in early childhood settings. She has been teaching within the local community, where many children are already familiar with her classroom, and has developed a reputation for maintaining a calm, organized, and well-structured environment.

In addition to her work locally, Mrs. Katz has previously taught at the Pre-1A level within an established educational setting. This experience allows her to guide children through the transition into their first year of Cheder with clarity and consistency.

Her classroom approach is steady and structured, helping children feel comfortable while learning to function within clear expectations.
